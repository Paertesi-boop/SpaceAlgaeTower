# SpaceAlgaeTower v1.2

**Open Source Closed-Loop Microalgae Nutrition Paste Machine | Extreme Environment Edition**  
**開放源碼閉環微藻營養泥製造機｜極端環境生存版**

### Project Overview / 專案簡介

SpaceAlgaeTower is a vertical cylindrical, fully automatic closed-loop microalgae system designed for off-grid living and extreme enclosed environments such as Antarctic research stations, space stations, and Mars bases.

It uses microalgae as the primary biological component to produce edible lactic-acid fermented nutrition paste, while performing oxygen generation and CO₂ absorption. Version v1.2 introduces an external Water-Energy Recovery Module to improve water recirculation.

**Important Reality Check**: Complete energy and water self-sufficiency is extremely difficult. The system relies primarily on natural sunlight + solar panels, with a Stirling engine as auxiliary recovery. All performance data are conservative estimates for single-person use. Long-term operation still requires external inputs.

### Main Specifications / 主要規格

- **Diameter / 直徑**: 130–140 cm  
- **Height / 總高**: 180–200 cm  
- **Culture Volume (Layer 1) / 培養液量**: 650–800 L  
- **Daily Nutrition Paste Output / 每日營養泥產量**: 800–1000 g wet weight (25–35% solids, \~200–250 g dry weight)  
- **Total Weight / 總重量**:  
  - Empty (without contents) / 不含內容物: \~650–750 kg  
  - Fully loaded (with culture medium and biomass) / 含培養液與藻泥: \~1.1–1.3 metric tons  
- **Daily Water Replenishment / 每日補水需求**: 0.2–0.5 L (system operational loss, **excluding human drinking water**)  
- **Power Consumption / 耗電量**:  
  - With sunlight: \~3.5–5.0 kWh/day  
  - Without sunlight: \~5.8–7.5 kWh/day  

### Working Principle / 工作原理

The system consists of the main tower and an external Water-Energy Recovery Module:

1. Layer 1 cultivates microalgae to absorb CO₂ and produce oxygen and biomass.  
2. Layer 2 concentrates the microalgae into paste.  
3. Layer 3 performs lactic acid fermentation to produce shapeable nutrition paste.  
4. The external module processes wastewater and CO₂ via Sabatier reaction to generate water and methane.  
5. Methane is sent to Layer 4 for combustion or Stirling engine support, with produced water returned to the system.

The loop is **not 100% closed**. Unavoidable losses occur due to evaporation, minor leaks, and permanent human metabolic water loss.

### Losses & Limitations / 損耗與限制

- **Water Loss**: 0.2–0.5 L per day for system operation (mainly evaporation and minor leaks). Human drinking water is **not** included.  
- **Power Consumption**: See specifications above. Even with Stirling and methane combustion, full off-grid operation is not achievable without external support.  
- **Important Note**: Water from Layer 1 is **not suitable for direct drinking**. It contains live microalgae and metabolites that may cause gastrointestinal discomfort. A separate drinking water treatment system is required if potable water is needed.
