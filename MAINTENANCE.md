# Maintenance, Repair, Safety & Notes | 維護、維修、安全性、注意事項

### Safety / 安全性
- The methane combustion chamber must be equipped with CO sensors and automatic shut-off devices.
- All piping joints must be regularly inspected for leaks.
- Wear appropriate safety goggles and gloves when operating or maintaining the system.
- Do not disassemble high-pressure or high-temperature components while the system is running.

### Important Notes / 注意事項
- Culture pH must be strictly maintained between 7–9.
- Use external power during initial startup to stabilize the system.
- **Water from Layer 1 is NOT suitable for direct drinking**. It contains live microalgae and metabolites that may cause gastrointestinal discomfort. A separate drinking water treatment system is required if potable water is needed.
- Long-term operation still requires periodic replenishment of micronutrients and small amounts of pure water.

### Maintenance Schedule / 維護需知
- **Daily**: Check pH, dissolved oxygen, temperature; replenish micronutrients as needed.
- **Weekly**: Clean filters and inspect the water ring pump.
- **Monthly**: Check Sabatier catalyst and activated carbon filters.
- **Every 3–6 months**: Full inspection of Stirling engine, battery health, and all piping seals.

### Repair Guide / 維修指南
- Gas leak → Immediately stop the system and inspect all connections.
- pH anomaly → Check CO₂ supply and culture condition.
- Pump clogging → Perform backwash procedure or disassemble for cleaning.
- Stirling engine failure → Refer to manufacturer documentation or replace the module.
